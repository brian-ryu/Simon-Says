How to play

Each round the board will play part of a sequence of colour flashes. Afterwards you are to play back the sequence of colour flashes in the exact order that they were played.
More and more of the sequence will be played each round.
If you mess up, the sequence will play again and you'll get another chance. But if you play in Strict Mode this opportunity is not available to you.
